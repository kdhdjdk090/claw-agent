# Business Strategy Skill Pack

## Financial Modeling
- **Unit Economics**: LTV, CAC, LTV:CAC ratio (>3x), payback period (<12 months)
- **Revenue Models**: SaaS (ARR/MRR), usage-based, marketplace (GMV + take rate), transactional
- **Burn Rate**: Monthly cash burn. Runway = Cash / Monthly Burn. Raise when 6+ months left
- **Cohort Analysis**: Revenue retention by signup month. Net Revenue Retention >100% = expansion

## Product Strategy
- **PMF Signals**: Retention curve flattens (not declining to zero), NPS >40, organic growth
- **Prioritization**: RICE (Reach × Impact × Confidence / Effort). ICE for experiments
- **Roadmap**: Now (committed) → Next (planned) → Later (exploring). No dates on "Later"
- **Pricing**: Value-based, not cost-plus. Van Westendorp for willingness-to-pay research

## Fundraising
- **Deck Structure**: Problem → Solution → Market → Traction → Team → Ask
- **Metrics That Matter**: Growth rate, retention, unit economics, runway, revenue
- **Due Diligence Prep**: Clean cap table, data room ready, financial model auditable
- **Term Sheets**: Valuation, liquidation preference, board seats, pro-rata, anti-dilution

## Organizational Design
- **Team Topology**: Stream-aligned teams own end-to-end. Platform teams enable them
- **Hiring**: Culture fit is necessary but insufficient. Assess for the actual work
- **1:1s**: Weekly. 50% their agenda, 30% coaching, 20% context sharing
- **OKRs**: 3-5 objectives per quarter. Each with 2-4 measurable key results. 70% achievement = right difficulty

## Go-to-Market
- **PLG (Product-Led Growth)**: Free tier → activation → expansion → enterprise
- **Sales-Led**: SDR → AE → CS pipeline. Define MQL/SQL criteria clearly
- **Community-Led**: Build community around the problem, not the product
- **Launch Playbook**: Pre-launch (waitlist, beta), launch day (Product Hunt, HN), post-launch (momentum)
