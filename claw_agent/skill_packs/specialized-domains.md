# Specialized Domains Skill Pack

## Healthcare / HIPAA
- PHI (Protected Health Information): Encrypt at rest + transit. Audit all access
- Minimum necessary: Only access/store PHI needed for the specific function
- BAA (Business Associate Agreement): Required before any PHI handling
- HIPAA Security Rule: Administrative, physical, and technical safeguards
- Clinical data: FHIR for interoperability. HL7 for legacy systems

## Regulatory & Compliance
- **SOC 2**: Trust Service Criteria — Security, Availability, Processing Integrity, Confidentiality, Privacy
- **GDPR**: Consent-based processing, right to deletion, DPO, data minimization, DPIA for high-risk
- **ISO 13485**: Medical device QMS — design controls, risk management, post-market surveillance
- **ISO 27001**: ISMS — risk assessment, control implementation, internal audits, management review
- **PCI-DSS**: Cardholder data protection, network segmentation, encryption, access control

## Supply Chain & Logistics
- Demand forecasting: Statistical baselines + promotional lift + seasonal adjustment
- Safety stock: Service level target × demand variability × lead time variability
- Carrier management: Scorecards (on-time, damage, cost), lane-based allocation
- Customs: HS classification, Incoterms, FTA utilization, restricted party screening

## Finance & Accounting
- Revenue recognition: ASC 606 / IFRS 15. Performance obligations, variable consideration
- Financial modeling: Three-statement model (P&L, balance sheet, cash flow) linked
- Unit economics: CAC, LTV, payback period, contribution margin per segment
- Audit trail: Immutable transaction log. Double-entry. Reconciliation procedures

## Quality Management
- CAPA: Corrective and Preventive Action. Root cause analysis → fix → verify effectiveness
- Non-conformance: Detect → Classify → Investigate → Disposition → Prevent recurrence
- SPC: Statistical Process Control. Control charts, Cp/Cpk, out-of-control rules
- Internal audit: Plan → Execute → Report → Follow-up. ISO 19011 guidelines
