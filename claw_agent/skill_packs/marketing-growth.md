# Marketing & Growth Skill Pack

## Copywriting Frameworks
- **PAS**: Problem → Agitation → Solution. Lead with the pain
- **AIDA**: Attention → Interest → Desire → Action. Classic funnel
- **BAB**: Before → After → Bridge. Show the transformation
- **4Ps**: Promise → Picture → Proof → Push. For landing pages
- Headline rules: Specific beats clever. Numbers work. Address the reader directly

## SEO
- **Technical**: Sitemap, robots.txt, canonical tags, hreflang, Core Web Vitals
- **On-page**: Title tag (60 chars), meta description (155 chars), H1 matches intent, internal links
- **Content**: Topic clusters with pillar pages. Answer the user's actual question
- **Schema markup**: JSON-LD for Product, Article, FAQ, HowTo, Organization
- **AI SEO**: Structure as Q&A, cite sources, use machine-parseable formats

## Content Strategy
- Topic clusters: Pillar page + 8-15 cluster articles + internal links
- Content audit: Map every page to a keyword. Kill pages with no traffic and no links
- Publishing cadence: Consistency > frequency. 2 great posts beat 10 mediocre ones
- Distribution: Create once, repurpose N times (blog → thread → LinkedIn → newsletter)

## Email Marketing
- Welcome sequence: 5-7 emails over 14 days. Value-first, pitch later
- Subject lines: 40-60 chars. Personalized. Curiosity or benefit-driven
- Segmentation: By behavior (clicked, opened, purchased), not just demographics
- Metrics: Open rate (20%+ baseline), CTR (2.5%+), conversion rate, unsubscribe rate

## Paid Advertising
- Google Ads: Intent-based. Match keywords to search intent. Negative keyword discipline
- Meta/Facebook: Interest + lookalike targeting. Creative fatigue = swap every 2 weeks
- LinkedIn: B2B. Job title + company size + industry targeting. High CPM, high intent
- Retargeting: Segment by funnel stage. Different creative for awareness vs consideration

## Conversion Rate Optimization
- Hypothesis-driven: "If we [change], then [metric] will [direction] because [reason]"
- Test one variable. Statistical significance before calling winner
- Page speed matters: Every 100ms of load time = ~1% conversion drop
- Social proof: Testimonials, logos, case studies, numbers close to the CTA
- Forms: Minimize fields. Multi-step beats single long form. Progress indicator
